Title: Transitioning to Lmod.

This talk will focus on transitioning to Lmod from other Environment
Module Systems and how easy it is to start using Lmod.  The first part
will discuss how to start testing Lmod on your system and how to
deploy it in a gradual way.  Then we will discuss features of Lmod
that can enhance your users' and support teams experience.  Finally,
we will talk the software hierarchy and what it can do for you.
